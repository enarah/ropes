# ROPES Product Brief

## Name

**ROPES — Ranger Operations Platform for Enarah Services**

## Purpose

ROPES is Enarah's single-point dashboard for ranger operations, remote field delivery and partner organisation support. It brings together trips, vehicles, ranger teams, projects, reporting, compliance, people, and Fulcrum field data into one secure, multi-organisation platform.

The platform should serve two purposes:

1. **Enarah internal operations** — a practical command centre for managing Enarah's work, people, partners, projects, field activity and reporting.
2. **Partner subscription modules** — reusable tools that can be packaged for Aboriginal corporations, ranger programs and partner organisations.

## Product positioning

ROPES should not replace specialist tools such as Fulcrum. Instead, it should connect operational tools and provide the management, reporting, governance and AI assistance layer around them.

Plain-language positioning:

> ROPES helps ranger programs manage trips, vehicles, projects, people, reporting and Fulcrum field data from one secure dashboard.

- Demo users for sales and training

## Core modules

### Overview

A role-based command dashboard showing current trips, vehicles, staff movements, approvals, reporting deadlines, recent field activity, open risks and quick actions.

### Organisations

A multi-tenant organisation area where Enarah can manage its own data and separate partner organisations.

### Ranger Operations

Program profiles, ranger teams, workplans, activities, field evidence, training, attendance, outputs and report-ready summaries.

### Trips

Journey management planning, approvals, itineraries, participants, emergency contacts, communications plans, vehicle allocations, checklists, daily check-ins and trip reports.

### Vehicles

Fleet register, bookings, drivers, pre-start checks, defects, odometer readings, service schedules, safety equipment and links to trips.

### Projects

Projects, funding streams, workplans, milestones, activities, deliverables, reporting periods and evidence.

### People

Users, staff profiles, roles, memberships, training records, contractor records and access permissions.

### Fulcrum

Connection manager, apps/forms catalogue, field records, maps, data quality checks, AI data assistant, app builder and sync settings.

### Reports

Trip reports, monthly ranger activity summaries, funding reports, board reports, vehicle usage reports, Fulcrum data quality reports and project summaries.

### Compliance

WHS registers, incident records, policy register, risk assessments, training/competency tracking and audit logs.

## Subscription-ready modules

2. Vehicle Booking and Pre-start Checks
3. Ranger Operations Dashboard
4. Fulcrum Companion
